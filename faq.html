Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions from shippers and carriers.

For Shippers

How much does it cost to use Black Forge Logistics as a shipper?

It’s completely free. Shippers can onboard, post loads and manage shipments at no cost. We earn fees from carriers, not shippers.

How do I post a load?

After onboarding, you’ll be redirected to your personal dashboard where you can submit load details—origin, destination, equipment type, weight and rate. Our AI takes care of matching your loads to qualified carriers.

How does AI improve my shipping operations?

Our AI engine predicts the best carriers for your loads, optimizes routes for cost and sustainability, and provides real‑time tracking and analytics. This reduces delays, empty miles and carbon emissions.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use industry‑standard encryption and comply with transportation and privacy regulations. Your information is never sold to third parties.

For Carriers

Why do I need a subscription?

Subscriptions keep the platform free for shippers and fund our AI innovation. An active plan unlocks full load board access, advanced analytics and premium features.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards via Stripe. After choosing a plan, you’ll be directed to a secure Stripe checkout to complete payment.

What happens after I subscribe?

Once payment is processed, your carrier profile is activated. You’ll gain immediate access to the load board, receive AI‑based load recommendations and see premium analytics.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, subscriptions are month‑to‑month and can be cancelled from your dashboard. You’ll retain access until the end of your billing cycle.

What do Pro and Enterprise plans include?

Pro includes priority access to premium loads, dynamic pricing insights, fuel discounts, predictive maintenance alerts and dedicated support. Enterprise adds custom integrations, API access, private load boards, advanced forecasting and a dedicated account manager.
