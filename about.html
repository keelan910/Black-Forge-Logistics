About Black Forge

Learn about our history, mission and the values that guide our work

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by a team of logistics veterans and data scientists, Black Forge set out to reinvent an industry plagued by inefficiency and fragmentation. Our founders saw an opportunity to leverage artificial intelligence and software engineering to transform how freight moves around the world. What began as a small start‑up has quickly become a leader in next‑generation supply chain technology.

From our headquarters in Texas, we serve customers across North America, seamlessly connecting shippers with qualified carriers and delivering data‑driven insights that drive smarter decisions.

Our Mission

To harness the power of artificial intelligence to deliver frictionless, transparent and sustainable logistics solutions that empower shippers and carriers to thrive.

Customer Obsession

We put our customers first, listening to their needs and building technology that makes their businesses better. Every feature we develop starts with the user in mind.

Innovation & Integrity

We believe that innovation must be paired with responsibility. Our AI models are built ethically, our data is protected and our partnerships are transparent.
