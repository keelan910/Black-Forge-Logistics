Our Services

From advanced route planning to sustainable transport solutions, explore the innovations that set Black Forge apart.

AI Route Optimization

Our proprietary algorithms analyze millions of data points – including traffic patterns, weather and road restrictions – to create the most efficient routes for your shipments. Expect shorter transit times, lower fuel costs and happier drivers.

Predictive Analytics

Anticipate demand, capacity and market fluctuations with our comprehensive analytics suite. Our models forecast lane pricing, predict seasonal trends and detect anomalies, giving you unprecedented visibility into the future of your supply chain.

Carbon Tracking & Sustainability

Automatically calculate the carbon footprint of every shipment and receive recommendations to reduce your impact. Our green lane program leverages alternative fuels and optimized loads to drive sustainability forward.

Digital Twin Simulation

Create a digital replica of your supply chain to test scenarios virtually. Simulate capacity changes, lane closures and unexpected demand surges before they happen to make better decisions in the real world.

Autonomous Fleet Management

Our platform integrates seamlessly with autonomous and semi‑autonomous vehicles. Monitor performance, remotely control operations and ensure regulatory compliance – all from a single dashboard.

Smart Pricing & Load Matching

Our dynamic pricing engine ensures competitive rates for shippers while maximizing earnings for carriers. Pair your available capacity with the best loads using real‑time market data and AI‑driven matching algorithms.

API & Platform Integration

Connect seamlessly with your existing TMS, ERP or warehouse software using our robust APIs and webhooks. Automate tendering, invoicing and reporting with ease.

24/7 AI Chat Assistant

Get instant answers to your logistics questions anytime. Our intelligent assistant provides support, scheduling and market updates whenever you need it.

Load Aggregator & Scraping AI

Consolidate the best freight opportunities from across the web. Our intelligent scraper ingests data from vetted load boards and public listings and surfaces them in a single, easy‑to‑use interface. Stay ahead of the competition with comprehensive, real‑time load visibility.
